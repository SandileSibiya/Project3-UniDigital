About CPUT Uni Digital

Uni Digital is mobile application designed and programmed by student for student to make academic life for many CPUT student easier, CPUT equips thousands of students every year with the skills to thrive in an ever-changing world. It is a hub for creativity, problem-solving, and digital transformation.

The Mission

Our mission is to create an application equiped with digital card for student to use everyday instead of physical card. At CPUT and many other institutions, outdated systems and slow processes make students waste valuable time. No digital alternative means you’re stuck without access to campus facilities until that plastic card is in your hand. The result? Frustration, delays, and a campus experience that feels more stressful than it should be.

Our Digital Solution

We believe students deserve better. That’s why we’re creating a modern web-based application that makes student card services faster, smarter, and digital-first. With our system, students can apply online, access a digital card instantly, and collect their physical card later — no more wasting time in queues.

It’s more than just convenience. This solution reduces wait times, empowers students with a flexible digital identity, and helps the university scale without investing in more printers or physical infrastructure. It’s about making student life simpler, smoother, and ready for the future.
